Sakthi Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Sakthi is a women safety and emergency support app. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and how users can contact us about privacy questions.

Information We Collect

Depending on the features you use, Sakthi may collect or process:

  • Account information such as name, username, phone number, and login details.
  • Emergency contact details added by the user.
  • Location information for SOS alerts, live tracking, safe routes, and nearby help features.
  • SOS alert details, including time, approximate location, and alert status.
  • Optional media evidence such as images, audio, or related emergency evidence submitted by the user.
  • Community safety reports submitted by the user.
  • Device push notification tokens used to send safety alerts.
  • Basic technical information needed to operate and secure the app.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide and improve Sakthi features, including:

  • Creating and managing user accounts.
  • Sending SOS alerts to emergency contacts.
  • Sharing live location links during emergency or tracking sessions.
  • Helping users find safer routes and nearby places.
  • Displaying community safety reports.
  • Sending push notifications related to safety and emergency alerts.
  • Maintaining app security, debugging issues, and improving reliability.

App Permissions

Sakthi may request the following permissions:

  • Location: to support SOS alerts, live tracking, safe routes, and nearby help.
  • SMS: to support emergency messaging features when enabled by the user.
  • Camera: to allow users to capture optional SOS evidence.
  • Microphone: to allow users to record optional SOS evidence or use voice SOS features.
  • Notifications: to send safety alerts and emergency-related updates.
  • Internet: to connect the app to backend services and maps.

Sharing of Information

Sakthi shares information only as needed to provide safety features. For example, SOS alerts and live tracking links may be shared with emergency contacts selected by the user. We do not sell personal information. Information may also be shared if required by law or to protect users, the public, or the security of the service.

Data Storage and Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. Users should share sensitive information only when necessary for safety features.

User Choices

Users can choose whether to grant app permissions. Some features may not work if required permissions are denied. Users can update or remove emergency contacts in the app where supported.

Children's Privacy

Sakthi is intended for users who can safely and lawfully use personal safety and emergency communication features. We do not knowingly collect information from children without appropriate consent.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised last updated date.
